Use AI to pick and ban like the pros

In an effort to defeat the Paladins, we, the Magistrate, have developed an AI tool that will predict the best champion to pick/ban given a ranked roster. It's 100% open source, free forever, and open to community contribution!

What we do + How we do it

Historical Data

We train AI models on historical match data on Paladins. (More info on amount of data used coming soon)

Ranked Roster

We take in the current ranked roster (partials accepted) of both teams to identify the best possible counter pick/ban.

Always Improving

We periodically update our models as new data becomes available.

Community Based

Fully Open-Source and accepting community contributions.
